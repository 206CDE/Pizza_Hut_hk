About Us

Our Brand

  Founded on June 15, 1958, Pizza Hut is famous for its successful pizza brand. Till now on, Pizza Hut has over 18,703 restaurants around the globe, which makes it the largest pizza chain in the world. 

Our Team

For more than 23,000 employees in our team, we serve our dear customer with our loalty and fidelity. We care about our customer more than anything, and we believe we can give them the most impressive experience. 

In Hong Kong

The first Pizza Hut in Hong Kong is opened in 1981. Until now, Pizza Hut has been serving Hong Kong for over 30 years in more than 45 outleds located in Hong Kong Island, Kowloon, New Territories and Lantau Island.
